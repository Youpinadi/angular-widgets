Smart gauge

Smart gauge can be used in dashboards. It allows you to display a gauge, which will update automatically and change its color depending on tresholds you have set. You can have a labbeled jauge just by setting the "label" attribute. If you don't set any warning or danger treshold, the jauge will be blue.

<smart-gauge warning="{{ warningTreshold }}" danger="{{ dangerTreshold }}" value="30" percent="50" label="{{errorLabel}}">

Universal player

Universal player is a directive that enables you to embed any player with the same simple tag.

<player site="dailymotion" video-id="x567" width="320" height="200" >
<player site="youtube" video-id="x567" width="320" height="200" >
<player site="vimeo" video-id="x567" width="320" height="200" >
